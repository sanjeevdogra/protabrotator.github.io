Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

Pro Tab Rotator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

What the Extension Does

Pro Tab Rotator is a browser extension that automatically switches between browser tabs and refreshes them at specified intervals. It is intended for dashboards, kiosks, and other non-interactive display environments.

Permissions Used

  • tabs – Used to switch between open tabs.
  • storage – Used to store local user settings or apply GPO-managed configurations.
  • alarms – Used to trigger scheduled tab switching events.
  • host permissions – Used only to reload the currently visible tab during rotation.

No Data Collected

This extension does not collect, log, share, or sell any data about you, your devices, or your browsing activity. All actions occur locally within the browser.
